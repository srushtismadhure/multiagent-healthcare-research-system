## Bucket 4: AI in Healthcare

### High-Performing Queries
*(Populate after first runs)*

### Query Tips
- FDA AI device list: filter by "De Novo" or "510(k)" for clearances; "PMA" for approvals
- "ambient AI clinical documentation" returns better results than "AI transcription"
- STAT News has the best AI failure coverage — search their site specifically
