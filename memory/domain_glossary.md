# Domain Glossary — Healthcare Acronyms and Terms

**Purpose:** Reference for healthcare, policy, and HIT terminology used across research buckets.

---

## Regulatory and Policy

| Term | Definition |
|------|-----------|
| ACA | Affordable Care Act (Obamacare) — federal health insurance law |
| CMS | Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services — federal agency |
| CMMI | Center for Medicare and Medicaid Innovation — runs payment model experiments |
| EO | Executive Order |
| HIPAA | Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act — patient data privacy law |
| HHS | Department of Health and Human Services |
| OIG | Office of Inspector General |
| PFS | Physician Fee Schedule — annual CMS rule setting physician payment rates |
| 340B | Federal drug pricing program requiring manufacturers to offer discounts to safety-net providers |
| FMAP | Federal Medical Assistance Percentage — federal share of Medicaid costs |

---

## Payment Models and Reimbursement

| Term | Definition |
|------|-----------|
| ACO | Accountable Care Organization — group of providers sharing risk |
| ACO REACH | Realizing Equity, Access, and Community Health — updated ACO model |
| BPCI | Bundled Payments for Care Improvement — episode-based payment model |
| Capitation | Fixed per-member per-month payment regardless of services used |
| CMHC | Community Mental Health Center |
| CPT Code | Current Procedural Terminology — standardizes medical billing codes |
| DRG | Diagnosis-Related Group — hospital inpatient payment classification |
| FFS | Fee-for-Service — traditional per-service payment model |
| HCPCS | Healthcare Common Procedure Coding System — billing codes for non-physician services |
| HEDIS | Healthcare Effectiveness Data and Information Set — quality measures |
| MA | Medicare Advantage — private Medicare plans |
| MACRA | Medicare Access and CHIP Reauthorization Act |
| MIPS | Merit-based Incentive Payment System |
| PCMH | Patient-Centered Medical Home |
| Prior Auth | Prior Authorization — payer approval required before certain services |
| VBC | Value-Based Care — payment tied to outcomes rather than volume |

| ICD-10 | International Classification of Diseases, 10th Revision — diagnosis coding |

| PGHD | Patient-Generated Health Data |
